<p><p>C-Mounted Filters are optical filters designed to be used with cameras and imaging systems. Bandpass Filters are a type of C-Mounted Filter that allows a specific range of wavelengths to pass through, while blocking others. Longpass Filters, on the other hand, block shorter wavelengths and allow longer wavelengths to pass through. These filters are widely used in various applications such as fluorescence microscopy, machine vision, and spectroscopy to enhance image contrast and improve overall image quality.</p></p>
