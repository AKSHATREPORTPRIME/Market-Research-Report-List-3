<p><p>Cationic Aqueous Polyurethane is classified into two main types based on the functional group present in the polymer structure: Carboxylic Acid Type Polyurethane and Sulfonic Acid Type Polyurethane. Carboxylic Acid Type Polyurethane contains carboxylic acid groups, which provide enhanced adhesion properties, while Sulfonic Acid Type Polyurethane contains sulfonic acid groups, offering improved water resistance and durability. Both types of cationic aqueous polyurethane are widely used in various industries such as coatings, adhesives, textiles, and construction for their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
