<p><strong>The Antifreeze & Coolants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inorganic Acid Technology</li><li>Organic Acid Technology</li><li>Hybrid Organic Acid Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Antifreeze & Coolants market includes three main types: Inorganic Acid Technology (IAT), Organic Acid Technology (OAT), and Hybrid Organic Acid Technology (HOAT). IATs primarily consist of inorganic compounds like phosphates and silicates. OATs are made of organic acids such as carboxylic acids. HOATs combine both organic and inorganic compounds for improved protection. Each type offers unique benefits in terms of corrosion prevention, heat transfer, and compatibility with different materials. Companies choose the type that best suits their specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
