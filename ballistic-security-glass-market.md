<p><p>Ballistic Security Glass is available in various types such as Traditional Laminated Glass, Insulated Ballistic Glass, Acrylic, Polycarbonate, and Glass-clad Polycarbonate. Traditional Laminated Glass consists of multiple layers of glass bonded together with a polymer interlayer for protection against impact. Insulated Ballistic Glass includes a layer of air or gas between two panes of glass to enhance thermal insulation properties. Acrylic and Polycarbonate are materials known for their high impact resistance, while Glass-clad Polycarbonate combines the strength of polycarbonate with the aesthetic appeal of glass.</p></p>
