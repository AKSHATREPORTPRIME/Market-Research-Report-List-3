<p><p>Core Balance Current Transformers(BCTs) are primarily available in two market types: Split Type and Through Type. The Split Type BCTs are designed to be separated into two halves for installation around existing conductors, making them ideal for retrofit applications. On the other hand, Through Type BCTs feature a single-piece construction and are typically used in new installations where the conductor can pass through the center of the transformer. Both types of BCTs are essential for accurate measurement and protection in electrical systems.</p></p>
