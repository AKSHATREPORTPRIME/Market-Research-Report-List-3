<p><ul><li>Self-Regulating / Self-Limiting</li><li>Constant-Wattage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heat trace cables come in two main market types: self-regulating/self-limiting and constant-wattage. Self-regulating cables automatically adjust their heat output based on the surrounding temperature, making them energy-efficient and safe to use. Constant-wattage cables provide a consistent level of heat output regardless of the temperature, making them suitable for applications where a steady amount of heat is required. Both types of heat trace cables are commonly used in various industries such as oil and gas, chemical, and construction for maintaining the temperature of pipes and equipment.</p></p>
