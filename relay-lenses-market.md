<p><p>Relay lenses are used in optical systems to transfer an image from one location to another without significant distortion. There are two main types of relay lenses: Ordinary Spherical Lenses and Aspheric Lenses. Ordinary Spherical Lenses are traditional lenses with a curved surface, while Aspheric Lenses have a more complex shape that reduces spherical aberrations and improves image quality. Both types of relay lenses have their own advantages and applications in various industries such as photography, medical imaging, and telecommunications.</p></p>
