<p><p>Headliners made using thermoplastic materials are versatile, easily moldable, and can be reshaped multiple times. They are commonly used in automobiles and other applications where flexibility is needed. Thermoset headliners, on the other hand, are durable and rigid, making them ideal for applications where structural integrity is important. The choice between the two types of headliners often depends on the specific requirements of the end use, with thermoplastic headliners favored for their ease of customization and thermoset headliners preferred for their strength and stability.</p></p>
