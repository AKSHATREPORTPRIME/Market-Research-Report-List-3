<p><strong>The Tin Containers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Built-up Containers</li><li>Shallow Drawn Containers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tin containers market consists of two main types, built-up containers and shallow drawn containers. Built-up containers are formed by welding together separate pieces of tinplate, resulting in strong and stable structures suitable for packaging products that require robust protection. On the other hand, shallow drawn containers are created by stretching a single piece of tinplate over a mold, producing containers with a smooth and seamless surface. Shallow drawn containers are ideal for packaging products that require a sleek and elegant appearance.</p></p>
