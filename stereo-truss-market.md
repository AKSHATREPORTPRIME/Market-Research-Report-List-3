<p><p>Stereo truss markets can be categorized into two main types: Tetrahedron and Polyhedron. Tetrahedron trusses are composed of four triangular faces and are known for their stability and strength. They are often used in smaller structures or as bracing components within a larger truss system. Polyhedron trusses, on the other hand, feature multiple faces and are more versatile in terms of design and application. They are commonly used in larger structures such as concert stages, exhibition booths, and architectural installations.</p></p>
