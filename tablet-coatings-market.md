<p><strong>The Tablet Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Icing</li><li>(Half) Film Coat</li><li>Enteric Coat</li><li>Slow Release Garments</li><li>Controlled Release Garments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tablet coatings market types include icing, (half) film coat, enteric coat, slow release garments, and controlled release garments. Icing coatings provide a smooth and glossy finish to tablets. (Half) film coats protect tablets from moisture and mask any unpleasant taste or odor. Enteric coatings protect tablets from stomach acid and ensure they dissolve in the intestines. Slow release garments slowly release the active ingredient over time. Controlled release garments release the active ingredient at a predetermined rate for optimal effectiveness.</p></p>
