<p><ul><li>BOPP</li><li>PVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Phosphorescent tapes are available in two main types - BOPP and PVC. BOPP phosphorescent tapes are made from bi-axially oriented polypropylene and are known for their high tensile strength and durability. PVC phosphorescent tapes, on the other hand, are made from polyvinyl chloride and are more flexible and offer good resistance to moisture and chemicals. Both types of tapes are widely used in various industries for marking, safety, and visibility purposes due to their glow-in-the-dark properties.</p></p>
