Dual tube detonators contain two separate shock tubes for added safety and redundancy, allowing for more controlled detonation sequences. Single tube detonators have only one shock tube and are commonly used in simpler blasting applications where precision timing is not as critical. Both types are used in mining, construction, and demolition industries to initiate explosives safely and efficiently.</p></p>
