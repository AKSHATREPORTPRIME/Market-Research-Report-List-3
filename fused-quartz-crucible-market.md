<p><p>Fused Quartz Crucible market can be classified into two types based on clarity: Clear and Clearless. Clear fused quartz crucibles have high optical clarity and are preferred for applications where visibility is important, such as in research and laboratory settings. On the other hand, Clearless fused quartz crucibles have lower optical clarity and are commonly used in industrial applications where visual inspection is not required. Both types of crucibles offer excellent thermal stability and chemical resistance, making them suitable for a wide range of high-temperature processes.</p></p>
