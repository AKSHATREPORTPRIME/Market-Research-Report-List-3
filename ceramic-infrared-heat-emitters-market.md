<p><p>Ceramic infrared heat emitters come in two main types: Medium Wave and Long Wave. Medium wave emitters emit infrared radiation with wavelengths between 2.5-4 microns, providing effective heat penetration for industrial applications like drying and curing. Long wave emitters emit infrared radiation with wavelengths between 5-10 microns, producing gentle and even heat distribution ideal for heating larger areas like warehouses or commercial spaces. Both types of emitters are widely used in various industries for their efficient heating capabilities.</p></p>
