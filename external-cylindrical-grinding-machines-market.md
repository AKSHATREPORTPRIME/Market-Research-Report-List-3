<p><strong>The External Cylindrical Grinding Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PLC-controlled</li><li>CNC-controlled</li><li>Manually-controlled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>External cylindrical grinding machines can be classified into three types based on their control system: PLC-controlled, CNC-controlled, and manually-controlled machines. PLC-controlled machines are equipped with programmable logic controllers to automate the grinding process, ensuring consistent and precise results. CNC-controlled machines are integrated with computer numerical control systems for efficient and complex machining operations. In contrast, manually-controlled machines rely on operators to adjust and operate the machine settings, offering more flexibility but potentially less accuracy compared to automated systems.</p></p>
