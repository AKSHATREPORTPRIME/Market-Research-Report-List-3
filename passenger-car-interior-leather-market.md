<p><p>Passenger car interior leather market includes two main types: Genuine Leather and Synthetic Leather. Genuine leather is made from animal hide and is known for its durability, luxury feel, and unique patina as it ages. On the other hand, synthetic leather, also known as faux leather, is made from artificial materials like polyurethane and offers a more cost-effective alternative to genuine leather. Both types of leather are commonly used in the automotive industry to create stylish and comfortable interiors for passenger cars.</p></p>
