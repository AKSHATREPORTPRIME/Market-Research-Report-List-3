<p><p>Internal Combustion Engine market can be divided into two main types: 2 stroke and 4 stroke engines. 2 stroke engines complete the intake, compression, power, and exhaust strokes in just two movements of the piston, making them more powerful but less efficient. On the other hand, 4 stroke engines complete these strokes in four movements, leading to better fuel efficiency and lower emissions. Both types cater to different requirements, with 2 stroke engines often being preferred for small, high-performance applications, and 4 stroke engines being more common in larger vehicles and machinery.</p></p>
