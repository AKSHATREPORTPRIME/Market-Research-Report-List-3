<p><strong>The Satphone Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Geosynchronous Satphone</li><li>Low-Earth Orbit Satphone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Satphone services are categorized into two main types: geosynchronous satphone and low-earth orbit satphone markets. Geosynchronous satphones are positioned at a fixed point above the Earth, allowing them to cover a larger area, such as an entire continent. They have a longer communication delay due to their higher altitude. On the other hand, low-earth orbit satphones are placed at lower altitudes, resulting in shorter communication delay and better call quality. However, they require a larger number of satellites to cover the same area.</p></p>
