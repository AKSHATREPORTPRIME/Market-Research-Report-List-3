<p><p>Textile binders are used in the textile industry to impart stability and strength to fabrics. The market for textile binders can be segmented into different types such as acrylic copolymer, polyvinyl acetate, vinyl-acrylate/acrylic, and styrene-acrylate. Acrylic copolymer binders are known for their high durability and resistance to chemicals. Polyvinyl acetate binders offer good adhesion properties. Vinyl-acrylate/acrylic binders provide excellent flexibility and low-temperature resistance. Styrene-acrylate binders are valued for their water resistance and adhesion to various substrates. Each type of binder serves different purposes in the textile industry.</p></p>
