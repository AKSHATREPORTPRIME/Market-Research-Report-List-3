<p><p>Mid-and High-Level Precision GPS Receivers can be categorized into Single Frequency and Dual Frequency types based on the number of frequencies they can receive. Single Frequency receivers are capable of receiving signals from one GPS frequency, providing moderate accuracy. On the other hand, Dual Frequency receivers can receive signals from two GPS frequencies simultaneously, resulting in higher accuracy and precision. Dual Frequency receivers are typically more expensive but offer better performance in challenging environments with sources of signal interference.</p></p>
