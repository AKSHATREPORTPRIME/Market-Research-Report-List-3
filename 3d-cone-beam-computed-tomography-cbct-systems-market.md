<p><p>3D Cone Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT) Systems market offers two main types: Image Intensifier Systems and Flat-Panel Imager Systems. Image Intensifier Systems use a traditional X-ray image intensifier tube to convert X-rays into visible light and then into a digital image. On the other hand, Flat-Panel Imager Systems use amorphous silicon detectors to directly convert X-rays into digital signals. Both systems have their own advantages and limitations, catering to different preferences and requirements in the medical imaging industry.</p></p>
