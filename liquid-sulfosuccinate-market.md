<p><p>The liquid sulfosuccinate market is divided into two main types: mono-ester sulfosuccinate and di-ester sulfosuccinate. Mono-ester sulfosuccinate is a product derived from the reaction between succinic acid and a single alcohol, while di-ester sulfosuccinate is produced by the reaction between succinic acid and two alcohols. These two types of sulfosuccinate have different properties and applications in industries such as personal care, home care, and oilfield chemicals. Both mono-ester and di-ester sulfosuccinate are widely utilized surfactants due to their excellent wetting, dispersing, and emulsifying properties.</p></p>
