<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Furniture Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based</li><li>Water-Based</li><li>Solventless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Furniture adhesives can be classified into three main types based on their composition: solvent-based, water-based, and solventless adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives contain volatile organic compounds that help in bonding materials together. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly as they contain water as a solvent instead of harmful chemicals. Solventless adhesives do not contain any solvent and are considered to be the most environmentally friendly option. Each type of adhesive offers unique benefits and is suitable for different applications in the furniture industry.</p></p>
