<p><p>Medical plasma thawers are utilized in medical facilities to thaw frozen plasma units quickly and efficiently before transfusion to patients. There are two main types of plasma thawers available on the market: wet plasma thawers and dry plasma thawers. Wet plasma thawers use a water bath to thaw plasma units, while dry plasma thawers use a controlled heating system without the need for water. Both types of plasma thawers serve the same purpose of safely and effectively thawing plasma units for transfusion.</p></p>
