<p><p>Power supply isolation equipment can be classified into different types based on the insulation media used for isolation. Insulation media power supply isolation equipment includes devices that use materials like glass, ceramic, or resin to provide electrical isolation. Air gap power supply isolation equipment utilizes physical space or distance between components to prevent electrical conduction. Other types of isolation equipment may use methods such as electromagnetic or capacitive isolation. These variations in design offer different levels of isolation performance and can be chosen based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
