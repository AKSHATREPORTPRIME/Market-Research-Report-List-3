<p><p>Tetrahydrofuran is produced through various methods in the market. One common method is the dehydration of 1,4-Butanediol, where 1,4-butanediol is converted into THF. Another method is the Maleic Anhydride Method, where maleic anhydride is reacted with 1,3-butadiene to form THF. The Furfural Method involves the hydrogenation of furfural to produce THF. Other methods may include the use of different starting materials or catalysts to generate Tetrahydrofuran. Each method has its own advantages and applications in the market.</p></p>
