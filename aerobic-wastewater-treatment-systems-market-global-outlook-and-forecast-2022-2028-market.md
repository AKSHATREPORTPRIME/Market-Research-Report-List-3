It includes different types of systems such as Conventional Activated Sludge, Moving Bed Biofilm Reactor (MBBR), and Membrane Bioreactor (MBR). Conventional Activated Sludge involves using microorganisms to break down organic matter in wastewater, while MBBR utilizes a biofilm with microorganisms attached to a floating media. MBR incorporates a membrane filtration system to separate treated water from the biomass. These systems are used to treat wastewater and remove pollutants before the water is discharged or reused.</p></p>
