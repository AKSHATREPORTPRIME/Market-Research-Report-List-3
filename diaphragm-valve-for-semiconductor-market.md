<p><p>Diaphragm valves for semiconductor applications come in two main types: manual valves and automatic valves. Manual valves require manual intervention to control flow, making them suitable for applications where frequent adjustments are not needed. On the other hand, automatic valves are equipped with actuators that enable them to open or close automatically based on preset conditions. Automatic valves are ideal for applications that require precise and continuous flow control without the need for human intervention. Both types play a crucial role in the semiconductor industry for ensuring efficient and accurate fluid handling.</p></p>
