<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The 3D Medical Printing Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stereolithography (SLA)</li><li>Digital Light Processing (DLP)</li><li>Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM)</li><li>Selective Laser Sintering (SLS)</li><li>Electronic Beam Melting (EBM)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stereolithography (SLA) uses a UV laser to solidify liquid resin layer by layer. Digital Light Processing (DLP) is similar to SLA but uses a digital light projector to cure the resin. Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) melts and deposits thermoplastic material in layers. Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) fuses powdered material using a laser. Electronic Beam Melting (EBM) uses an electron beam to melt and fuse metal powders. Other types of 3D medical printing systems include inkjet and binder jetting technologies.</p></p>
