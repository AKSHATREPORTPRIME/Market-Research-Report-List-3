<p><p>Semi-automatic vacuum packaging machines are available in two main types: Vertical Form Fill Seal (V-FFS) and Horizontal Form Fill Seal (H-FFS). V-FFS machines are designed to package products in a vertical position, making them ideal for packaging items such as powders, granules, and liquids. On the other hand, H-FFS machines package products in a horizontal position, making them suitable for packaging larger items such as meats, cheeses, and whole fruits. Consumers can choose the type of machine based on the size and shape of the products they need to package.</p></p>
