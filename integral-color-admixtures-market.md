<p><p>Integral color admixtures are used in construction to add color to concrete. There are two main types of integral color admixtures: powder and liquid. Powder admixtures are added to the concrete mix in dry form, while liquid admixtures are pre-mixed and can be easily added to the concrete. Both types offer a wide range of colors and shades to choose from, allowing for customization of the concrete's appearance. The powder market is preferred for its ease of use and long shelf life, while the liquid market offers convenience and consistency in color mixing. Both types cater to different preferences and applications in the construction industry.</p></p>
