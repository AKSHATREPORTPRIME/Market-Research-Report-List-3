PCB EDA focuses on designing and manufacturing circuit boards used in various electronic devices. IC EDA involves designing and testing integrated circuits used in electronic components. SIP EDA involves the development and licensing of reusable semiconductor IP blocks, such as cores, to be integrated into custom IC designs.</p></p>
