<p><ul><li>Headlight</li><li>Taillight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bike lights market can be divided into two main types: headlight and taillight. Headlights are installed at the front of the bike and are crucial for illuminating the road ahead, improving visibility and safety for the rider. Taillights, on the other hand, are mounted at the back of the bike and are essential for making the rider visible to others on the road, especially in low-light conditions. Both types of lights play a vital role in ensuring the safety of cyclists during nighttime or low-visibility conditions.</p></p>
