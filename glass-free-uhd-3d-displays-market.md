<p><p>Glass-free UHD 3D Displays come in two primary market types: Parallax Barrier Technology and Lenticular Display Technology. The Parallax Barrier Technology displays use a barrier layer that creates 3D effects by controlling the light emitted from the display. On the other hand, Lenticular Display Technology uses a series of lenses to separate the left and right images, providing a 3D effect without the need for specialized glasses. Both market types offer high-quality, immersive 3D viewing experiences without the use of traditional 3D glasses.</p></p>
