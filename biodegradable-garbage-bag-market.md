<p><p>Biodegradable garbage bags come in various types such as Polylactic acid (PLA), Polyhydroxyalkanoate (PHA), Starch Blends, and others. PLA bags are made from renewable resources like corn starch and sugarcane. PHA bags are produced by bacteria and can break down in various environments. Starch blend bags are a mix of corn, potato, or wheat starch. Other types of biodegradable bags may include cellulose-based materials. These options provide eco-friendly alternatives to traditional plastic bags, reducing environmental impact.</p></p>
