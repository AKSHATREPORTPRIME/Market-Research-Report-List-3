<p><p>A clinical tissue expander is a medical device that is utilized in reconstructive surgeries to expand and stretch soft tissues. It is used primarily in breast reconstruction after a mastectomy or in other procedures where there is a need for additional tissue. The expander is often placed beneath the skin and gradually inflated with saline solution over time, allowing the surrounding tissues to adapt and expand. Once the desired tissue expansion is achieved, the expander is removed, and a permanent implant can be inserted.</p><p>In recent years, the clinical tissue expander market has witnessed steady growth, mainly due to the increasing prevalence of breast cancer and an increasing number of reconstructive procedures performed.
