<p><p>Printed Circuit Boards (PCBs) are used in electronic devices to provide mechanical support and electrical connections. They come in three main types - Single-sided, Double-sided, and Multi-layer. Single-sided PCBs have components and connections on one side of the board, Double-sided have components and connections on both sides, and Multi-layer PCBs have multiple layers of conductive material sandwiched between insulating layers, allowing for more complex circuits and thinner boards. Each type serves different purposes and is used based on the complexity and requirements of the electronic device.</p></p>
