<p><strong>The Fiber Optics Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Mode Fiber Optics Cable</li><li>Single-Mode Fiber Optics Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber optics cable market is categorized into two types: Multimode Fiber Optics Cable and Single-Mode Fiber Optics Cable. Multimode cables have larger cores and are used for short-distance transmissions within buildings or campuses. They allow multiple light rays to travel simultaneously, resulting in lower transmission speeds. On the other hand, single-mode cables utilize thin cores and facilitate long-distance transmissions, making them suitable for telecommunications and internet applications. Due to their higher cost and performance, single-mode cables are typically used in long-haul communication networks.</p></p>
