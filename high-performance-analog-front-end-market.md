<p><p>High-performance analog front end (AFE) market includes two main types: 3-channel AFE and 6-channel AFE. A 3-channel AFE is designed to handle signals from three different inputs simultaneously, making it ideal for applications requiring data collection from multiple sources. On the other hand, a 6-channel AFE can handle signals from six different inputs simultaneously, providing even greater flexibility and efficiency in capturing and processing data. Both types of AFEs cater to the demand for high-performance signal processing solutions in various industries.</p></p>
