<p><p>Polyether Diamines are versatile compounds used in various industries such as adhesives, coatings, and elastomers. They are categorized based on their backbone structure - PPG Backbone and PEG Backbone. PPG Backbone Polyether Diamines are derived from propylene oxide and ethylene oxide, offering high thermal stability and chemical resistance. On the other hand, PEG Backbone Polyether Diamines are derived from polyethylene glycol, providing excellent flexibility and low-temperature properties. Both types cater to different applications and market demands within the polyether diamines industry.</p></p>
