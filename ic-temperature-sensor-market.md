<p><p>The IC temperature sensor market includes various types such as voltage output temperature sensors, current output temperature sensors, digital output temperature sensors, resistance output silicon temperature sensors, and diode temperature sensors. Voltage output sensors provide temperature information in the form of a voltage signal, current output sensors use a current signal, while digital output sensors offer temperature readings in a digital format. Resistance output silicon sensors measure temperature based on the change in electrical resistance, and diode sensors use the voltage change across a diode to determine temperature.</p></p>
