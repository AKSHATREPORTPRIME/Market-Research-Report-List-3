<p><p>Metal adhesive market can be classified into three main types based on the composition of the adhesive - acrylics based, silicone based, and others. Acrylics based adhesives offer high adhesion strength and temperature resistance, making them suitable for various metal bonding applications. Silicone based adhesives, on the other hand, provide flexibility and resistance to extreme temperatures. Other types of metal adhesives include epoxies and polyurethanes, offering different properties such as fast curing times and high chemical resistance. Each type of adhesive caters to specific needs in the metal bonding industry.</p></p>
