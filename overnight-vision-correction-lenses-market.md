<p><p>Overnight Vision Correction Lenses, also known as orthokeratology or Ortho-k lenses, come in two main market types: Boston Material and Paragon Material. Boston Material lenses are made from a rigid gas-permeable material, which allows more oxygen to reach the eyes compared to traditional rigid lenses. Both types of lenses are used for overnight wear to correct vision while sleeping.</p></p>
