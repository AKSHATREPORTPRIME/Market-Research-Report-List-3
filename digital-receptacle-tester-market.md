<p><p>Digital receptacle testers are essential tools for ensuring electrical safety in residential, commercial, and industrial settings. The two main types in the market are the GFCI receptacle tester and the AFCI receptacle tester. GFCI receptacle testers are designed to test for ground fault circuit interrupter functionality, which helps prevent electrical shock. AFCI receptacle testers, on the other hand, are used to test for arc fault circuit interrupter functionality, which helps prevent electrical fires. Both types of testers are crucial for maintaining electrical safety standards and should be used regularly.</p></p>
