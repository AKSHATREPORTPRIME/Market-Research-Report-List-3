<p><p>Webcam software market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based webcam software operates on remote servers and allows users to access and manage their webcam footage through the internet. It provides flexibility, scalability, and remote access capabilities. On the other hand, on-premise webcam software is installed and runs on local servers or computers, enabling users to control and access their webcam footage within their own premises. It offers greater control over data security and may suit organizations that prioritize privacy and have limited internet connectivity.</p></p>
