<p><p>Cleanroom cables refer to cables that are specifically designed for use in cleanroom environments where there is a need to maintain high cleanliness levels and prevent the release of contaminants. Single core cables are cables that consist of a single conducting core and are commonly used for power transmission applications. On the other hand, multi core cables are cables that have multiple conducting cores, usually bundled together, and are widely used for transmitting multiple signals or data. Both types of cables are crucial in cleanroom settings to ensure efficient and reliable communication and power transmission.</p></p>
