<p><p>NdFeB Permanent Magnets (Neodymium-iron-boron) are widely used in various industries due to their strong magnetic properties. There are two main types of NdFeB magnets: Sintered NdFeB Magnet and Bonded NdFeB Magnet. Sintered NdFeB magnets are made through powder metallurgy, resulting in high magnetic strength and brittleness. On the other hand, Bonded NdFeB magnets are manufactured by mixing NdFeB powder with a polymer binder, offering lower magnetic strength but higher flexibility in shapes and sizes. Both types cater to different market demands and applications.</p></p>
