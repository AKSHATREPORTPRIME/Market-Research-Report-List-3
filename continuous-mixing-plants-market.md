<p><p>Continuous mixing plants are classified into two main types: stationary continuous mixing plants and mobile continuous mixing plants. Stationary continuous mixing plants are fixed installations that are typically used for large-scale production of concrete or asphalt mixtures in one location. On the other hand, mobile continuous mixing plants are portable, allowing for on-site production of concrete or asphalt mixtures as needed. Both types of plants offer efficient and continuous mixing capabilities, but cater to different needs based on the scale and mobility requirements of the project.</p></p>
