<p><p>Antirusting agents are categorized into three main types based on their solubility: Water Soluble Antirust Agents, Oil Soluble Antirust Agents, and Emulsion Type Antirust Agents. Water soluble antirust agents are easily soluble in water and provide protection against rust when applied to metal surfaces. Oil soluble antirust agents are dissolved in oil and are effective in preventing corrosion on metal parts. Emulsion type antirust agents combine the properties of both water and oil soluble agents, offering versatile protection against rust on various metal surfaces.</p></p>
