<p><p>Phototherapy apparatus market can be divided into three types: Conventional, LED, and Fiberoptic. Conventional phototherapy uses fluorescent or incandescent lamps to emit light, while LED phototherapy utilizes light emitting diodes for a more concentrated and controlled treatment. Fiberoptic phototherapy uses fiber optic cables to deliver light directly to the patient's skin. Each type of phototherapy apparatus offers unique advantages and is used for treating various skin conditions such as jaundice in newborns or skin disorders like psoriasis.</p></p>
