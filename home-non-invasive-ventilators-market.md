<p><p>Home non-invasive ventilators are medical devices that assist patients with breathing difficulties in their own homes. There are two main types of home non-invasive ventilators: Single Level Ventilators and Bilevel Ventilators. Single level ventilators provide a constant level of air pressure, while bilevel ventilators deliver two different levels of air pressure - one for inhalation and one for exhalation. These devices are beneficial for patients with conditions such as sleep apnea, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease, and neuromuscular disorders.</p></p>
