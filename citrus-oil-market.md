<p><p>Citrus oil refers to the essential oil extracted from the fruits of various citrus plants, including orange, lemon, lime, mandarin, and grapefruit. The growing consumer demand for natural and organic products, along with the increasing awareness regarding the health benefits of citrus oil, has been driving the market growth.</p><p>In the food and beverages industry, citrus oil is used as a flavoring agent in soft drinks, alcoholic beverages, confectionery, and dairy products. It is also utilized in the production of emulsifiers and natural sweeteners. In the cosmetics and personal care sector, citrus oil is a common ingredient in perfumes, soaps, shampoos, and skincare products due to its refreshing and uplifting aroma. Moreover, citrus oil has antimicrobial, antioxidant, and anti-inflammatory properties, making it sought after in the pharmaceutical industry for medicinal purposes.</p><p>The market for citrus oil is expected to continue its growth trajectory in the coming years.
