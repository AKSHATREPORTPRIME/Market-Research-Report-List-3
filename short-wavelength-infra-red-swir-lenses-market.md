<p><p>Short Wavelength Infra-Red (SWIR) lenses are available in two main market types: prime lens and zoom lens. Prime lenses have a fixed focal length, offering high image quality and maximum light transmission. They are commonly used for applications requiring constant magnification. On the other hand, zoom lenses have variable focal lengths, allowing users to adjust the magnification levels. This versatility makes zoom lenses ideal for applications where different magnification levels are needed, such as surveillance and remote sensing.</p></p>
