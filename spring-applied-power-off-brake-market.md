<p><p>Spring Applied Power Off Brakes consist of two main types in the market- Dualspring Brake and Monospring Brake. The Dualspring Brake utilizes two separate springs to provide the necessary braking force when power is turned off. On the other hand, the Monospring Brake uses a single spring for the same purpose. Both types are commonly used in various industrial applications where immediate stopping power is needed in the event of power failure, ensuring safety and preventing accidents.</p></p>
