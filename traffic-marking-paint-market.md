<p><p>Traffic marking paint, thermoplastic, and preformed polymer tape are three types of materials used for marking roads and parking lots. Traffic marking paint is a liquid paint that is typically applied with a sprayer and dries quickly to create crisp lines. Thermoplastic is a heated material that is melted onto the surface and provides durability and reflective properties. Preformed polymer tape is a pre-cut tape that is laid down and heat-sealed to the surface, offering a quick and easy way to mark roads. Each type has its own advantages and applications in the traffic marking industry.</p></p>
