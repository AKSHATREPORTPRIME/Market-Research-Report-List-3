<p><p>Polypropylene fibers are commonly categorized into two main types: long fibers and staple fibers. </p><p>Long fibers are continuous strands of polypropylene that are typically used in applications where strength and durability are essential, such as in the production of ropes, geotextiles, and automotive parts. </p><p>Staple fibers, on the other hand, are shorter lengths of polypropylene that are often used in non-woven fabrics and textiles due to their softer feel and drapability. Both types of polypropylene fibers offer a wide range of applications and benefits in various industries.</p></p>
