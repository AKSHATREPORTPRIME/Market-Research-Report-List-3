<p><p>Closed-Loop Ground Source Heat Pump systems are categorized into three main market types based on the source of heat exchange fluid: Horizontal, Vertical, and Surface Water. Horizontal systems use a network of pipes buried in shallow trenches, Vertical systems use deep boreholes to circulate fluid, and Surface Water systems extract heat from nearby bodies of water. Each market type offers unique advantages and considerations in terms of installation, efficiency, and environmental impact.</p></p>
