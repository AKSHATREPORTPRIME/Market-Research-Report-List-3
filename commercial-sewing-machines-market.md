<p><p>Commercial sewing machines come in two main types: manual sewing machines and electric sewing machines. Manual sewing machines are operated by hand, using a foot pedal to control the stitching speed. They are commonly used for smaller-scale projects or for those who prefer a more traditional sewing experience. On the other hand, electric sewing machines are powered by electricity, allowing for faster stitching speeds and more precise stitching. They are typically used for larger-scale projects or for those who require more advanced features and technology in their sewing machine.</p></p>
