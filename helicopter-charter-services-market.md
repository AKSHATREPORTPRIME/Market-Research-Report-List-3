<p><p>Helicopter charter services market is divided into two main types: single-engine helicopters and twin-engine helicopters. Single-engine helicopters are smaller, more compact, and usually less expensive to operate. They are ideal for short distances, personal use, and sightseeing tours. On the other hand, twin-engine helicopters are larger, more powerful, and offer greater capacity and range. They are commonly used for executive transport, aerial photography, emergency medical services, and offshore oil rig support. The choice between single-engine and twin-engine helicopters depends on factors such as budget, distance, capacity, and specific requirements of the charter service.</p></p>
