<p><ul><li>Fiber Yarn</li><li>Fiber Roving</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrical fiberglass market types include fiber yarn and fiber roving. Fiber yarn consists of twisted or spun fibers that are used in applications such as electrical insulation, textiles, and reinforcements. Fiber roving, on the other hand, is a bundle of untwisted continuous fibers that are used in processes like filament winding and pultrusion. Both fiber yarn and fiber roving play a crucial role in the electrical fiberglass industry by providing strength, durability, and insulation properties to various products and applications.</p></p>
