<p><p>The System-on-Chip (SoC) market encompasses three main types: digital, analog, and mixed signal. Digital SoCs focus on processing and transmitting digital signals efficiently, while analog SoCs specialize in processing analog signals such as sound, light, or temperature. Mixed signal SoCs combine both digital and analog components to interact with the physical world and process information more effectively. Each type caters to specific applications and requirements, offering a wide range of solutions for various industries such as consumer electronics, automotive, and IoT.</p></p>
