<p><p>PCR devices are used for replicating DNA sequences. Digital PCR devices provide absolute quantification of DNA samples by partitioning them into thousands of individual reactions. Real-time PCR devices, on the other hand, continuously monitor the amplification of DNA in real-time, allowing for the quantification of DNA during the amplification process. Both types of devices are crucial in molecular biology research, diagnostics, and various other applications in healthcare, forensics, and agriculture.</p></p>
