Hardness 80 A tubing is more rigid and abrasion-resistant, ideal for pneumatic tubing. Hardness 85 A offers a balance between flexibility and rigidity, suitable for general-purpose applications. Hardness 90 A tubing is the most rigid and is often used in high-pressure applications.
