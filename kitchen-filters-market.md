<p><p>Kitchen filters come in two main types: Duct Filters and Non-Duct Filters. Duct filters are installed in the kitchen exhaust system to trap grease and smoke, preventing them from circulating back into the kitchen. Non-duct filters, on the other hand, are standalone units that filter out odors and impurities without the need for ductwork. Both types of filters play a crucial role in maintaining a clean and healthy kitchen environment by improving air quality and reducing the risk of fire hazards.</p></p>
