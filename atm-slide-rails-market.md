<p><ul><li>Full Extension</li><li>Partial Extension</li><li>Over Extension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ATM Slide Rails come in three main market types: Full Extension, Partial Extension, and Over Extension. Full Extension slide rails allow for the drawer to be fully extended out of the ATM enclosure, providing easy access to the contents. Partial Extension slide rails allow for only a portion of the drawer to be extended out, limiting access to the contents. Over Extension slide rails go beyond full extension, providing maximum access to the drawer contents, often extending beyond the ATM enclosure for full visibility and convenience.</p></p>
