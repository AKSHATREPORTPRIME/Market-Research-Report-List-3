<p><p>Solar On Grid Inverters can be categorized into three main types based on their capacity and application. Micro inverters are designed for individual solar panels, converting DC electricity into AC electricity at the panel level. String inverters are used for small to medium scale solar systems, converting DC electricity from multiple panels into AC electricity. Central inverters are suitable for large-scale solar installations, converting DC electricity from numerous panels into AC electricity for feeding into the grid. Each type serves a specific market segment based on size and application.</p></p>
