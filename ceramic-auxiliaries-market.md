<p><p>Ceramic auxiliaries market types include rheology modifiers, defoamers, dispersants, wetting agents, and others. Rheology modifiers are used to control the flow and viscosity of ceramics during processing. Defoamers help in reducing foam formation in ceramic slurries. Dispersants enhance the dispersion of solids in a liquid medium. Wetting agents improve the wetting and spreading properties of ceramic materials. Other auxiliaries may include binders, plasticizers, and anti-settling agents, which also play a crucial role in the ceramic manufacturing process.</p></p>
