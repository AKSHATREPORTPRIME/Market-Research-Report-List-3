<p><p>Post-etch cleaning solutions are used in the semiconductor industry to remove residue left behind after the etching process. There are two main types of post-etch cleaning solutions: aqueous and semi-aqueous. </p><p>Aqueous solutions are water-based and environmentally friendly, making them a popular choice for many manufacturers. They are effective at removing particles and contaminants without leaving any harmful residues. </p><p>Semi-aqueous solutions combine water with organic solvents to provide a balance between performance and environmental impact. They are often used when aqueous solutions alone are not effective enough.</p></p>
