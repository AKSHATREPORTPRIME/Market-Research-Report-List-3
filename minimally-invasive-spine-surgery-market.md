One of the main drivers of this growth is the increasing prevalence of spinal disorders and injuries, including degenerative disc disease, herniated discs, spinal stenosis, and scoliosis. The growing aging population and rising adoption of sedentary lifestyles are contributing factors to the rising incidence of these conditions.</p><p>Technological advancements have also played a crucial role in the market's growth.
