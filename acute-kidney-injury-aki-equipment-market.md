<p><p>The Acute Kidney Injury (AKI) equipment market consists of various types of equipment used for the treatment of AKI. Continuous Blood Purification (CBP) is a type of renal replacement therapy that continuously filters and cleanses the blood to remove waste products and excess fluid. Hemodialysis (HD) is another form of renal replacement therapy that uses a dialyzer to filter the blood outside the body. The "Other" market category includes additional equipment and treatments such as peritoneal dialysis and renal replacement therapy using adsorbents. These different equipment options provide clinicians with diverse tools to address AKI based on patient needs.</p></p>
