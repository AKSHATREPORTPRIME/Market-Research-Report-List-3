<p><p>Silicon-based fingerprint sensors come in two main types: touch type and slide type. Touch type sensors require the user to place their finger directly on the sensor surface, while slide type sensors involve the user swiping their finger across the sensor. Touch type sensors are more commonly found in smartphones and tablets due to their convenience and speed, while slide type sensors are often used in security systems and access control applications where a higher level of security is required.</p></p>
