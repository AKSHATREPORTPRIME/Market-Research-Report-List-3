<p><p>Specialty Nitrile Butadiene Rubber (NBR) market consists of three main types - Carboxylated NBR, Bimodal NBR, and Liquid NBR. Carboxylated NBR is modified with carboxyl groups to enhance properties like adhesion and elasticity. Bimodal NBR has a unique polymer blend for improved performance in various applications. Liquid NBR is a solution polymerization process, resulting in a higher degree of chain branching and lower viscosity, making it suitable for applications requiring excellent oil and fuel resistance. Each type caters to specific industry needs and offers unique advantages.</p></p>
