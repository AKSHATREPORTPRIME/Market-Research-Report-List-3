<p><p>Solid State LiDAR sensors are revolutionizing the automotive industry with their advanced capabilities. There are three main types of Solid State LiDAR sensors in the market - MEMS Based Scanning, Phase Array, and Non-Scanning Flash. MEMS Based Scanning sensors use tiny mirrors to direct the laser beams for scanning, Phase Array sensors use an array of lasers for simultaneous scanning, and Non-Scanning Flash sensors use a flash of laser light to capture the entire scene at once. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in different industries.</p></p>
