<p><p>Therapeutic medical guide wires are used in minimally invasive procedures to navigate through blood vessels or other anatomical structures. Solid guide wires are made of a single piece of material, giving them more durability and flexibility. Wrapped guide wires have a coil structure wrapped around a core wire, providing enhanced flexibility and support for navigating complex anatomies. Both types of guide wires play crucial roles in guiding catheters and other medical devices during procedures, with each offering unique benefits in different clinical scenarios.</p></p>
