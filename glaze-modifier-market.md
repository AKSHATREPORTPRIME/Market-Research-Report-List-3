<p><strong>The Glaze Modifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Opalescence Glaze Modifier</li><li>Bentonite Glaze Modifier</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glaze modifiers are additives used to alter the properties of glazes in ceramics. Opalescence glaze modifiers are used to create a pearlescent or iridescent effect in glazes. Bentonite glaze modifiers are clay minerals that can thicken glazes and improve their suspension properties. Other types of glaze modifiers may include fluxes, colorants, or opacifiers that further enhance the appearance or performance of the glaze. Each type of modifier offers unique benefits and can be chosen based on the desired outcome in the final glazed product.</p></p>
