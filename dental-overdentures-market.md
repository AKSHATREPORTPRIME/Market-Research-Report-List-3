<p><p>Dental overdentures are a type of dental prosthesis that are designed to fit over natural teeth or dental implants. There are two main types of dental overdentures: complete overdentures and removable partial overdentures. Complete overdentures are used when all of the natural teeth are missing, and they are designed to cover the entire dental arch. Removable partial overdentures are used when some natural teeth are still present, and they are designed to fit around these remaining teeth. Both types of overdentures provide improved stability, function, and aesthetics compared to traditional dentures.</p></p>
