<p><p>Polyurethane thermally conductive adhesive market types include polyurethane thermally conductive potting compound and polyurethane thermally conductive gap filler. The potting compound is used for encapsulating electronic components to protect them from environmental factors while providing thermal conductivity. The polyurethane thermally conductive gap filler is utilized to fill gaps between heat-generating components and heat sinks in electronic devices to improve heat dissipation. Both products are essential for enhancing the thermal management of electronic systems.</p></p>
