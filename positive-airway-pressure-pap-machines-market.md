<p><p>Positive Airway Pressure (PAP) Machines Market consists of various types such as Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP), Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure (BiPAP), and Adaptive Servo Ventilation (ASV). CPAP machines deliver a constant level of air pressure to keep the airways open during sleep. BiPAP machines provide two different pressure levels for inhalation and exhalation. ASV machines adjust pressure levels based on the individual's breathing patterns. These machines are commonly used to treat sleep apnea and other respiratory disorders.</p></p>
