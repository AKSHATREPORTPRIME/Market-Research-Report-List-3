<p><strong>The Residential Portable Dehumidifiers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compressor Type Dehumidifiers</li><li>Desiccant Type Dehumidifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential Portable Dehumidifiers come in two main types - Compressor Type Dehumidifiers and Desiccant Type Dehumidifiers. Compressor Type Dehumidifiers work by drawing in air, cooling it to condense the moisture, and then reheating it before releasing it back into the room. On the other hand, Desiccant Type Dehumidifiers use a desiccant material to absorb moisture from the air, which is then heated to release the collected moisture. Both types are effective in reducing humidity levels in residential spaces.</p></p>
