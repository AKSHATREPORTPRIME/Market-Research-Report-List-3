<p><p>VOC Catalysts market can be categorized into three main types based on their physical structure: Pellet, Honeycomb, and Columnar. Pellet catalysts are small spherical particles, Honeycomb catalysts consist of a series of channels formed in a honeycomb structure, and Columnar catalysts are cylindrical in shape. Each type offers different advantages in terms of surface area, flow distribution, and catalytic efficiency. Companies may choose a specific type of catalyst based on their specific needs and requirements in controlling volatile organic compound emissions.</p></p>
