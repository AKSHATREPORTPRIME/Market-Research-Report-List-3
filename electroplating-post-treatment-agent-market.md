<p><p>Electroplating post-treatment agents are classified into three main types based on their pH levels: acidic, neutral, and alkaline. Acidic post-treatment agents are used for removing scale and rust from metal surfaces, while neutral agents are used for general cleaning and passivation. Alkaline post-treatment agents are typically used for removing oil and grease residues. Each type of agent is formulated to provide optimal results based on the specific requirements of the electroplating process and the material being treated.</p></p>
