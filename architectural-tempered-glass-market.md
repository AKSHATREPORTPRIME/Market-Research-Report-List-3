<p><strong>The Architectural Tempered Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Tempered Glass</li><li>Bent Tempered Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Architectural tempered glass market consists of two main types: flat tempered glass and bent tempered glass. Flat tempered glass is produced by heating and then quickly cooling the glass to increase its strength and durability, making it suitable for use in windows, doors, and facades. On the other hand, bent tempered glass is created by heating the glass to a specific temperature and then bending it into a desired curved shape before cooling, making it ideal for applications such as balustrades, canopies, and atriums.</p></p>
