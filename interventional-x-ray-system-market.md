<p><ul><li>Single Plane Interventional X – Ray System</li><li>Bi-plane Interventional X – Ray System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Interventional X-ray systems are used in minimally invasive procedures to diagnose and treat various medical conditions. Single plane interventional X-ray systems utilize a single C-arm for imaging, while bi-plane systems have two C-arms that can move independently, providing simultaneous imaging from different angles. Single plane systems are more common and cost-effective, while bi-plane systems offer advantages such as improved accuracy and reduced procedure times. Both systems play a crucial role in interventional radiology and cardiology procedures.</p></p>
