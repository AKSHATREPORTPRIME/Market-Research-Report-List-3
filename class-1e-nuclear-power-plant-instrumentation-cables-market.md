<p><p>The Class 1E Nuclear Power Plant Instrumentation Cables market includes two types: Nuclear Island Cables and Conventional Island Cables. Nuclear Island Cables are used to connect the instrumentation and control systems in the nuclear power plant's nuclear island. They are specifically designed to withstand harsh radiation and high-temperature environments. Conventional Island Cables, on the other hand, are used in the plant's conventional island, which includes systems like turbines and generators. These cables are not exposed to radiation but require durability and reliability for the efficient functioning of the power plant.</p></p>
