<p><p>The household refrigerator and freezer market can be classified into three main types based on the location of the freezer compartment: freezer on top, freezer on bottom, and freezer less. Freezer on top refrigerators are the most common type, with the freezer compartment positioned above the refrigerator section. Freezer on bottom refrigerators, on the other hand, have the freezer compartment located beneath the refrigerator section. Freezer less refrigerators do not have a separate freezer compartment, catering to consumers who prefer more refrigeration space over freezer storage.</p></p>
