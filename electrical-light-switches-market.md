<p><strong>The Electrical Light Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rheostat</li><li>Coil-rotation transformer</li><li>Autotransformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrical light switches can be categorized into three main types: rheostat switches, coil-rotation transformer switches, and autotransformer switches. Rheostat switches allow for variable control of the light intensity by adjusting the resistance in the circuit. Coil-rotation transformer switches use inductive coils to regulate the current flow and control the light intensity. Autotransformer switches adjust the voltage levels to control the brightness of the lights. Each type of switch offers unique advantages in terms of functionality and efficiency in the lighting market.</p></p>
