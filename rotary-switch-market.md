<p><p>Rotary switches come in various types, including single-deck, three-deck, four-deck, twelve-deck, and others. Single-deck rotary switches have one deck for controlling one circuit, while three-deck and four-deck switches have multiple decks for controlling multiple circuits. Twelve-deck rotary switches offer even more versatility by allowing control over twelve circuits. The "others" category includes unique rotary switch designs with different numbers of decks or specialized features to suit specific applications. Each type caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
