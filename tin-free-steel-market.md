<p><p>Tin Free Steel (TFS) can be categorized into two main types based on the coating material used - TFS-Cr and TFS-Ni. TFS-Cr refers to tin-free steel coated with chromium, which provides good corrosion resistance and adhesion properties. On the other hand, TFS-Ni refers to tin-free steel coated with nickel, offering superior corrosion resistance and barrier protection. Both TFS-Cr and TFS-Ni are widely used in manufacturing metal packaging for food and beverages, as well as other industrial applications.</p></p>
