<p><ul><li>Porous Wood Fiber Board</li><li>Hard Wood Fiber Board</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wood fiber boards come in two main types: Porous wood fiber boards and hard wood fiber boards. Porous wood fiber boards are lightweight and possess good thermal and acoustic insulation properties. They are commonly used in applications where moisture resistance is not a priority. On the other hand, hard wood fiber boards are denser and more durable, making them suitable for applications where strength and durability are key factors. Both types of wood fiber boards have their own unique characteristics and market demand based on specific requirements.</p></p>
