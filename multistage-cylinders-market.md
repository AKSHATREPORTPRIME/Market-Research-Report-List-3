<p><p>Multistage Cylinders refer to hydraulic or pneumatic cylinders that have multiple stages or sections within the cylinder body. </p><p>Single-Acting Cylinders are cylinders that use hydraulic or pneumatic pressure to extend the piston, but rely on external forces like gravity or springs to retract the piston.</p><p>Double-Acting Cylinders are cylinders that use hydraulic or pneumatic pressure to both extend and retract the piston, providing more control and versatility in applications. </p><p>Both types of cylinders are widely used in various industries for lifting, pressing, and positioning applications.</p></p>
