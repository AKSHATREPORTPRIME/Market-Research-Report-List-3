<p><strong>The Waterproof Breathable Fabrics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waterproof breathable fabrics are classified into Type I and Type II in the market. Type I fabrics are laminated membranes with pores that are smaller than water droplets, preventing water from entering while allowing moisture vapor to escape, making them highly waterproof and breathable. Type II fabrics have a durable water repellent (DWR) coating on the outer surface that prevents water from saturating the fabric, but may not be as breathable as Type I. Both types cater to different market needs based on their functional properties.</p></p>
