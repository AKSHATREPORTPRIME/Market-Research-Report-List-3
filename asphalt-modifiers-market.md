<p><p>Asphalt modifiers can be classified into three main types: physical modifiers, chemical modifiers, and other modifiers. Physical modifiers alter the physical properties of the asphalt, such as elasticity and viscosity, to improve its performance. Chemical modifiers, on the other hand, chemically react with the asphalt to enhance its durability, adhesion, or resistance to aging. Other modifiers may include additives like polymers or fibers that provide specific benefits to the asphalt mixture. All these modifiers aim to enhance the overall quality and longevity of asphalt pavements.</p></p>
