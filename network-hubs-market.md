<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Network hubs can be classified into two main market types: active and passive. Active hubs are powered and can regenerate data signals, allowing for longer cable runs and better signal quality. Passive hubs, on the other hand, do not require power and simply serve as connecting points for devices within a network. Active hubs tend to be more expensive but offer higher performance, while passive hubs are more cost-effective for simpler network setups. Both types have their own advantages and are suitable for different network requirements.</p></p>
