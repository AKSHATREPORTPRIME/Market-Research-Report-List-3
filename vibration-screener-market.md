<p><p>Vibration screeners are divided into two main types: Circular Vibrating Screeners and Linear Vibrating Screeners. Circular vibrating screeners are traditionally used for sieving bulk materials in industries such as mining and construction, due to their high efficiency and compact design. On the other hand, linear vibrating screeners are more commonly used in food and chemical processing industries for their ability to provide accurate particle separation and high throughput. Both types of screeners are essential equipment for various industrial applications.</p></p>
