<p><p>Product serialization software is used to track and trace products throughout the supply chain to ensure authenticity and prevent counterfeiting. </p><p>1-2 level serialization software allows manufacturers and distributors to assign unique identification codes to products and track them at the batch or lot level.</p><p>3 level serialization software enables tracking at the individual unit level, making it easier to detect and prevent counterfeiting.</p><p>4 level serialization software extends the tracking to pallets and cases, providing a more detailed view of product movement.</p><p>5 level serialization software goes beyond the supply chain and includes end-to-end visibility, from manufacturing to the point of sale, improving inventory management and customer experience.</p></p>
