Manufacturers are also focusing on integrating advanced technologies like beamforming and MU-MIMO (Multi-User, Multiple-Input, Multiple-Output) to enhance the performance of Wi-Fi boosters and cater to the growing demand for faster and more stable internet connections.</p></p>
