<p><p>Multi-strand anchor systems are commonly used in both tensile and compressive markets. In the tensile market, these anchors are designed to withstand pulling or stretching forces, such as in bridges, dams, and retaining walls. On the other hand, in the compressive market, multi-strand anchors are utilized to resist pushing or compressing forces, such as in deep foundations, tunnels, and underground structures. Both types of markets require multi-strand anchors to provide stability and structural support in various construction projects.</p></p>
