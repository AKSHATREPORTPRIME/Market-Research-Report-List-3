<p><ul><li>Silicon-based Adhesive</li><li>Acrylic-based Adhesive</li><li>Rubber-based Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Double sided masking tape is available in three main types based on the adhesive used: silicon-based, acrylic-based, and rubber-based. Silicon-based adhesive offers high-temperature resistance and is ideal for electronics and automotive applications. Acrylic-based adhesive provides good adhesion to a variety of surfaces and is commonly used in the construction industry. Rubber-based adhesive offers excellent flexibility and is often used for bonding irregular surfaces. Each type of adhesive has its own unique properties and applications in the market.</p></p>
